04 — Product Perspective

What 9 years inside
claims taught me about
insurtech products.

01

Liability Tools Miss the Real Blocker

The delay in liability determination is rarely the tool. It is upstream data availability. Insureds do not answer the phone. Police reports take days. Non-standard carriers have high-risk customers who are unreachable by design. A liability tool that does not account for these realities will see low adoption regardless of how accurate its algorithm is.

02

Adjuster Workarounds Break Migrations

Every adjuster has workarounds built around the limitations of their current system. When you migrate to new software without mapping those workarounds first, you do not just create a tech problem. You create a behavioral adoption problem. The best implementations took time at initiation to understand how people actually work, not just how the process is documented.

03

Training Cannot Compete With Caseload

Pulling adjusters off active files for full-day training sessions does not work. Files do not pause. Deadlines do not move. The most effective implementations broke training into short, workflow-specific segments mapped directly to daily adjuster tasks, not general platform overviews that disconnect from the reality of the job.
